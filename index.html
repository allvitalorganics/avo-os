Marketing

$10K budget · organic does acquisition, paid does defense

Total budget for the Salad Soymilk launch: $10K (~₩9.6M). The strategy is one sentence. Acquisition is free — organic, influencer friends, 공구, press. Paid is defensive — retargeting and brand keywords. The reserve deploys only after Week 2-4 once we know what's working.
